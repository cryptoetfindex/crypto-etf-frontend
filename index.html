CETFi Chain Testnet

CETFi Chain is a pioneering tokenized stock market blockchain as a network service (BaaNS), revolutionizing decentralized trading and analytics. Our dual orderbook liquidity pool model combines traditional orderbook trading with automated liquidity pools, ensuring efficient price discovery and robust market depth. This hybrid approach supports tokenized stocks, such as StockA (AlphaTech Solutions), by balancing high-frequency trading with stable liquidity provision. AI-powered features enhance trading strategies, optimize node performance, and provide predictive analytics, enabling seamless participation for retail and institutional investors. Join the testnet to explore decentralized trading, financial report uploads as NFT-like assets, and compliance-ready infrastructure.

Compliance and KYC

A large majority of activity on CETFi Chain is tracked for compliance with KYC and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) laws. Rewards are based on node uptime, and running a node is required to hold a mainnet wallet. Vendor onboarding will occur after testnet completion. No real money is transferred on the testnet, and there is no guarantee of any kind from testnet use. Everything is in the test phase and subject to change without notice. Any illegal activity will result in an IP address ban, so use caution.
