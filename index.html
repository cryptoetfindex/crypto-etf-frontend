Compliance Requirements

  • SEC Registration: Provide CIK and EDGAR URL (e.g., Form S-1, Form D).

  • KYC/AML: Upload PDF for identity and sanctions verification.
  • Partnership Contract: Sign and upload notarized text file.
  • Smart Contract: Provide valid address (e.g., ERC-20, SPL).
