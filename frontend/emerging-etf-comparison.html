Quick Verdict

  • Fastest Growing / Momentum Play: XRP ETFs – Unmatched inflow streak and $1B+ milestone make this the hottest emerging category.

  • Tech/Performance Bet: Solana ETFs – Higher potential upside tied to ecosystem growth, but more volatile flows.
  • Overall Recommendation: Allocate small portions (1-5% of portfolio) to emerging ETFs alongside core BTC/ETH exposure for balanced risk/reward.
