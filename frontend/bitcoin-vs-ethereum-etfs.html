Quick Verdict

  • Choose Bitcoin ETFs if: You want the most liquid, established crypto exposure with "digital gold" characteristics and larger market cap stability.
  • Choose Ethereum ETFs if: You believe in higher growth potential from smart contracts, DeFi, and future staking yields, and are comfortable with slightly higher volatility.
  • Best Overall Strategy: Diversify with both – many investors allocate 60-80% Bitcoin ETFs and 20-40% Ethereum ETFs for balanced exposure.
  • Current Momentum (Dec 2025): Ethereum showing stronger inflows and relative strength amid Bitcoin profit-taking.
