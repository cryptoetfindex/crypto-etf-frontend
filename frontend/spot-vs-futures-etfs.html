Why Spot ETFs Dominate in 2025

  • • Superior Tracking: No futures roll decay – returns match underlying crypto price more closely.
  • • Lower Costs: Expense ratios 60-80% cheaper than most futures products.
  • • Better Capital Efficiency: No collateral or margin requirements.
  • • Broader Selection: Spot versions now exist for Ethereum, XRP, and others – futures limited mostly to BTC.
  • • Investor Preference: Capital has overwhelmingly shifted from futures (e.g., ProShares BITO) to spot products since 2024 launches.

When Might Futures ETFs Still Make Sense?

  • • Leveraged/inverse exposure (e.g., 2x Bitcoin) – only available via futures structure.
  • • Certain tax or structural preferences in specific accounts (rare).
  • • Short-term tactical trading where roll yield is favorable (uncommon).

For nearly all long-term investors: Spot ETFs are clearly superior in 2025.
