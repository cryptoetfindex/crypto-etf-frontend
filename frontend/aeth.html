Strategy Overview

AETH uses quantitative trend signals to allocate between:

  • • Ethereum exposure (via futures) when trends are positive
  • • U.S. Treasuries (safe-haven ETFs) when trends are negative or uncertain

Goal: Capture ETH upside while reducing downside risk during bearish periods.
