How ETHI Works

ETHI seeks leveraged long exposure to Ethereum while generating additional income through options strategies (primarily covered calls or similar).

  • Amplified upside participation in ETH rallies
  • Income generation to offset leverage decay and costs
  • Complex dual strategy – leverage + options overlay
  • High volatility and potential for significant losses

Warning: Combines leverage risk with options complexity – suitable only for experienced investors.

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Leveraged upside in ETH bull markets
  • Additional income from options
  • Potential outperformance in moderate uptrends
  • Professional strategy management

Cons

  • Leverage decay in sideways/volatile markets
  • Options cap further upside
  • Very high risk of significant losses
  • Small AUM and limited liquidity

Who Should Consider ETHI?

  • Sophisticated traders seeking leveraged ETH exposure with income
  • Short- to medium-term bullish investors comfortable with high risk
  • Those understanding both leverage decay and options strategies
  • Active managers using tight risk controls

Not suitable for long-term buy-and-hold or risk-averse investors.
