Pros

  • • True 50/50 exposure – reduces Bitcoin dominance bias
  • • Simple balanced approach to the two largest cryptos
  • • No need to manually rebalance between BTC and ETH
  • • From established leveraged ETF provider ProShares

Cons

  • • Higher expense ratio than single-asset spot ETFs
  • • Futures-based – potential tracking differences vs physical holdings
  • • Very small AUM and low liquidity
  • • May underperform market-cap weighted funds when BTC outperforms ETH

How to Buy BETE

Available on major brokers including Fidelity, Vanguard, Charles Schwab, Robinhood, and Interactive Brokers. Search ticker "BETE". Suitable for investors preferring equal-weight exposure to Bitcoin and Ethereum over market-cap weighting.
