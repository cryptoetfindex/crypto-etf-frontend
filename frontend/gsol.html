How GSOL Works

Grayscale Solana Trust ETF holds actual SOL tokens in cold storage. Originally launched as a private trust, converted to ETF structure in 2025.

  • Direct spot SOL exposure
  • Grayscale's long track record in crypto trusts
  • Institutional-grade custody
  • No staking rewards (pure price tracking)
