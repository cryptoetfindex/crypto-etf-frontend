Pros

  • • Longest track record in Ethereum exposure (since 2017).
  • • High liquidity and wide availability on brokers.
  • • Familiar to legacy Ethereum investors from pre-ETF era.
  • • Established brand in the crypto trust space.

Cons

  • • Highest expense ratio at 2.50% – much higher than competitors (0.15–0.25%).
  • • Ongoing outflows as capital shifts to cheaper options.
  • • No competitive advantage on fees or features.
  • • Same Ethereum volatility risk as peers.

How to Buy ETHE

Available on nearly all major brokers including Fidelity, Vanguard, Charles Schwab, Robinhood, and interactive brokers. Search ticker "ETHE". Often retained by long-term holders from the trust days.
