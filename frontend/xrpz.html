How XRPZ Works

Franklin XRP ETF provides direct, physically-backed exposure to XRP tokens held in institutional-grade cold storage. The fund tracks the spot price of XRP with minimal tracking error.

  • No derivatives – holds actual XRP
  • Backed by Franklin Templeton’s 75+ years of asset management experience
  • Competitive fees and strong liquidity
  • Regulated structure with robust custody and reporting
