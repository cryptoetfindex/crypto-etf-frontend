Pros

  • • Competitive 0.25% expense ratio aligned with major leaders.

  • • Strong institutional appeal and global brand recognition.

Cons

How to Buy BRRR

Available on major brokers including Fidelity, Vanguard, Charles Schwab, Robinhood, and interactive brokers. Search ticker "BRRR". Appeals to investors seeking European crypto expertise in a U.S. ETF wrapper.
