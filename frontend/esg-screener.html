ESG Scoring Methodology

Our ESG ratings are based on a hypothetical scoring system considering:

  • Environmental: Blockchain energy consumption, carbon footprint, and sustainable mining practices (e.g., proof-of-stake vs proof-of-work).
  • Social: Community impact, accessibility, and inclusivity of the underlying technology.
  • Governance: Decentralization, transparency, and regulatory compliance of the ETF and asset.
