Quick Verdict

  • Best Overall / Most Popular: ETHA – Dominates in AUM, liquidity, and inflows.
  • Best for Fidelity Customers: FETH – Seamless integration and strong performance.
  • Lowest Fees: ETH Mini Trust (0.15%) – Ideal for long-term holders focused on minimizing costs.
  • Avoid Unless Legacy: ETHE – High 2.50% fee drives outflows to cheaper alternatives.

All funds provide nearly identical exposure to spot Ethereum price (minus fees). The main differences come down to cost, liquidity, and issuer trust.
