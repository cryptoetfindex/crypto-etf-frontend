How ORO Works

Arrow Valtoro ETF provides balanced exposure to both physical gold (via trusts/ETFs) and Bitcoin, aiming to combine the stability of precious metals with crypto growth potential.

  • Hybrid portfolio of gold and Bitcoin holdings
  • Rebalanced to maintain target allocation
  • Diversification across traditional and digital safe-haven assets
  • Institutional custody for both asset classes

Pros & Cons

Pros

Who Should Consider ORO?

  • Those wanting diversification in alternative assets
  • Portfolios hedging against inflation and market uncertainty
  • Conservative crypto entrants preferring blended risk
