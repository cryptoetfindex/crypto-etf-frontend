Portfolio Structure & Holdings (as of late 2025)

BITS is actively managed with a small number of holdings (~7). It gains blockchain exposure primarily through the affiliated Global X Blockchain ETF (BKCH) and Bitcoin exposure via U.S.-listed Bitcoin futures contracts (often rolled).

Pros

  • • Combined exposure to Bitcoin (via futures) and blockchain companies in one fund.
  • • Active management for futures roll optimization.
  • • Accessible pre-spot ETF Bitcoin strategy vehicle.
  • • Potential diversification within crypto ecosystem.

Cons

  • • Futures rolling can cause tracking drag vs spot Bitcoin.
  • • Higher expense ratio and smaller AUM/liquidity.
  • • Double-layer fees (BITS + underlying BKCH).
  • • Outflows and underperformance relative to pure spot Bitcoin ETFs in 2025.

How to Buy BITS

Available on major brokers including Fidelity, Vanguard, Charles Schwab, Robinhood, and Interactive Brokers. Search ticker "BITS". Suitable for investors seeking blended blockchain + Bitcoin futures exposure.
