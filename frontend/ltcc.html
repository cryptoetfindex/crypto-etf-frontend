How LTCC Works

Canary Litecoin ETF provides direct, physically-backed exposure to LTC tokens held in secure custody. The fund tracks the spot price of Litecoin with minimal tracking error.

  • No derivatives – holds actual LTC
  • Focus on established proof-of-work altcoin
  • Moderate fees and growing liquidity
  • Institutional-grade custody
