How BSOL Works

Bitwise Solana Staking ETF holds actual SOL tokens and stakes them to earn network rewards, passing yield through to shareholders (net of fees).

  • Direct spot SOL exposure + native staking rewards
  • Staking yield distributed periodically
  • Low fees and strong liquidity
  • Institutional-grade custody and security
