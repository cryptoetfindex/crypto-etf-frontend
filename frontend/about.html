About Crypto ETF Index

Your trusted, independent resource for spot crypto ETFs in 2025 and beyond.

Our Mission

Crypto ETF Index was created to provide clear, accurate, and unbiased information about the rapidly growing world of spot cryptocurrency ETFs. We help investors of all experience levels understand these products, compare options, track performance, and stay informed — all in one place.

What We Do

  • • Maintain up-to-date directories of all major spot Bitcoin, Ethereum, XRP, Solana, and multi-asset ETFs
  • • Publish in-depth reviews, comparisons, and educational guides
  • • Track daily inflows/outflows and report market-moving news
  • • Offer free tools including price widgets, flow trackers, and performance charts
  • • Explain complex topics (taxes, risks, regulation) in plain language

Editorial Standards & Independence

We are 100% editorially independent. We do not accept payment from ETF issuers or brokers to feature or rank products. Our content is researched and written by a team with deep experience in traditional finance and cryptocurrency markets.

All data is sourced from official issuer reports, regulated exchanges, and reputable third-party providers (SoSoValue, CoinGecko, ETFdb, Farside Investors, Bloomberg, etc.).
