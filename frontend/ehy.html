How EHY Works

EHY employs an aggressive covered call overlay on Ethereum holdings (via spot ETFs or futures) to generate the highest possible monthly income through premium collection.

  • Sells near-the-money or slightly out-of-the-money calls for maximum premium
  • Aims for ultra-high distribution yields (often >40% annualized)
  • Significant upside limitation – designed purely for income
  • Distributions paid monthly

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Extremely high monthly income potential
  • Strong performance in sideways/volatile markets
  • Professional aggressive option strategy
  • Monthly distributions for cash flow

Cons

  • Severe upside cap – misses most rallies
  • Higher risk of NAV erosion in declining markets
  • Very small AUM and limited liquidity
  • New fund with short track record

Who Should Consider EHY?

  • Aggressive income seekers prioritizing yield over capital appreciation
  • Investors expecting range-bound or moderately bearish ETH prices
  • Those using tax-advantaged accounts to maximize distributions
  • Portfolio diversifiers looking for high-yield alternative exposure
