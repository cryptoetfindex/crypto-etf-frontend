Yield Methodology

  • Staking ETFs: Based on current network staking rewards (e.g., Ethereum ~4%, Solana ~7%) minus ETF fees.
  • Income Funds: Hypothetical distributions from covered call strategies or lending.
  • Spot ETFs: Generally 0% yield unless specified (no staking pass-through).
  • Leveraged/Inverse: Typically 0% yield due to daily reset mechanics.
