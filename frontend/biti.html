Pros

  • • -1x daily inverse exposure – profits when Bitcoin falls.
  • • Useful short-term hedge against Bitcoin/long crypto positions.
  • • Established issuer ProShares with experience in inverse products.
  • • Decent volume for tactical trading.

Cons

  • • High risk – daily reset causes decay in long-term holds or sideways markets.
  • • 0.95% expense ratio – expensive for hedging.
  • • Losses amplified in Bitcoin rallies.
  • • Not suitable for long-term directional bets due to compounding effects.

How to Buy BITI

Available on major brokers including Fidelity, Vanguard, Charles Schwab, Robinhood, and interactive brokers. Search ticker "BITI". High-risk inverse product – only for experienced traders seeking short-term downside protection.
