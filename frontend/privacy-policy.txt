Crypto ETF Index Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 22, 2025

1. Introduction
Crypto ETF Index, LLC ("we", "us", or "Platform"), a Delaware limited liability company, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website (crypto-etf-frontend.pages.dev) or services for crypto ETF analytics and tokenized stock launchpads.

2. Information We Collect
a. Personal Information: When you submit a Company Registration form, we collect:
   - Company Name, EIN, SEC Registration Number, FINRA CRD Number, EDGAR Filing URL.
   - KYC/AML documents (PDF, <5 MB), signed partnership contract (text).
   - Email and message via the Contact Form.
b. Non-Personal Information: We collect analytics data (e.g., page views, click events) via cookies and similar technologies.
c. Blockchain Data: We verify smart contract addresses (e.g., ERC-20, SPL) for tokenized stocks, which are publicly accessible.

3. How We Use Your Information
a. To process Company Registration and verify SEC compliance.
b. To provide technical support and visualization services for tokenized stock launches.
c. To respond to Contact Form inquiries.
d. To improve our website and services through analytics.

4. Data Sharing
a. We do not sell or rent your personal information.
b. We may share data with:
   - Regulatory authorities (e.g., SEC, FINRA) to verify compliance.
   - Service providers (e.g., Cloudflare for hosting) under strict confidentiality.
c. Public blockchain data (e.g., smart contract addresses) is not considered personal information.

5. Data Security
a. We use industry-standard encryption (e.g., HTTPS) and secure storage.
b. KYC/AML documents and contracts are stored securely with restricted access.
c. Despite our efforts, no system is completely secure. You assume the risk of data breaches.

6. Your Rights
a. You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us.
b. We will respond within 30 days, subject to legal obligations (e.g., SEC recordkeeping).
c. You may opt out of cookies via browser settings, but this may limit functionality.

7. Third-Party Links
Our website may link to external sites (e.g., EDGAR, Etherscan). We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

8. Children's Privacy
Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on our website with the updated effective date.
