Crypto ETF Index: Revolutionizing Tokenized Stock Analytics

Abstract
Crypto ETF Index provides a platform for visualizing and managing crypto and tokenized stock portfolios. Launching July 24, 2025, we aim to bridge traditional finance and blockchain technology.

1. Introduction
The rise of tokenized assets offers new opportunities for investors. Our platform provides tools to track and analyze these assets.

2. Features
- Asset Distribution: Pie charts for portfolio visualization.
- Historical Data: Line, bar, and candlestick charts (Basic+).
- Portfolio Management: Track assets (Basic+).
- Price Alerts: Notifications for price changes (Basic+).
- Voice Integration: Voice commands (Pro+).
- Sharing: Share insights (Pro+).
- Company Registration: Enterprise analytics.

3. Technology
Built with JavaScript, Tailwind CSS, and Particles.js for an interactive UI. Data is sourced from public APIs.

4. Risks
Crypto and tokenized stocks are volatile. Users assume all risks. See our Risk Policy for details.

5. Conclusion
Crypto ETF Index empowers users with data-driven insights. Join us on July 24, 2025.
