How TXBC Works

21Shares FTSE Crypto 10 ex-BTC Index ETF tracks a market-cap weighted index of the largest 10 digital assets excluding Bitcoin, offering pure altcoin exposure.

  • Physically-backed basket of top altcoins
  • Rebalanced periodically to reflect leading non-BTC assets
  • 21Shares' global leadership in crypto ETPs
  • Institutional custody and transparency
