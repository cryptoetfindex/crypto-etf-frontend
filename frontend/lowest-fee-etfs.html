Why Fees Matter

  • • Even small differences compound significantly over time (e.g., 0.15% vs 1.50% on $10k over 10 years = thousands saved).
  • • Competition has driven fees down dramatically since 2024 launches.
  • • Lowest-fee funds often attract long-term, cost-conscious investors and steady inflows.
  • • Balance fees with liquidity and issuer trust – ultra-low fees on tiny funds may have wider spreads.

Recommendation

For most investors seeking low costs:

  • • Core Bitcoin: ARKB (0.21%) or BITB (0.20%)
  • • Core Ethereum: Grayscale Mini Trust (0.15%)
  • • Diversified: Multi-asset baskets around 0.59%
  • • Prioritize funds with strong AUM and liquidity alongside low fees.
