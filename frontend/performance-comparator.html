Understanding Crypto ETF Performance (2025)

1. Key Metrics Explained

  • Since Inception: Total return from ETF launch date to now.
  • YTD (Year-to-Date): Return from January 1, 2025 to current date.
  • 1 Year: Trailing 12-month return.
  • Tracking Error: How closely the ETF follows its underlying asset (typically low for spot ETFs).

2. Factors Affecting Performance

  • Underlying asset price (BTC/ETH).
  • Expense ratio and fees (subtract from returns).
  • Inflows/outflows impacting premium/discount to NAV.
  • Leveraged ETFs amplify daily returns (and losses) — not suitable for long-term holding.
  • Market volatility and liquidity.

3. Comparison Tips

  • Spot vs. Futures: Spot ETFs generally track price more accurately with lower tracking error.
  • Thematic ETFs: Higher volatility but potential for outperformance in bull markets.
  • Use different chart types for various perspectives: bar for direct comparison, radar for multi-dimensional view.
  • Consider both absolute returns and risk-adjusted metrics.
