Best Brokers for Crypto ETFs 2026

Informational comparison of popular U.S. brokerage platforms that support crypto ETFs (Bitcoin, Ethereum, XRP, Solana, multi-asset). Features, fees, retirement account availability, and considerations as of January 01, 2026.

This is not an endorsement or recommendation of any broker. All information is for educational purposes only.

Key Factors When Choosing a Broker for Crypto ETFs

  • • Supported ETFs: Availability of all major spot crypto ETFs (BTC, ETH, XRP, SOL, thematic).
  • • Commissions & Fees: $0 trades standard; watch for account minimums or inactivity fees.
  • • Retirement Accounts: Ability to hold crypto ETFs in IRAs/Roth IRAs (tax advantages).
  • • Tools & Research: Charts, screeners, educational resources.
  • • User Experience: Mobile app quality, ease of use.
  • • Customer Service: Support availability and reputation.
  • • Security: SIPC insurance (up to $500k), 2FA, cold storage partnerships.

Fidelity Investments

  • • Supports all major crypto ETFs (IBIT, FBTC, ETHA, FETH, XRP, Solana)
  • • $0 commissions on ETFs
  • • Excellent retirement account support (IRA, Roth, 401(k) rollover)
  • • Strong research tools and customer service
  • • Highly rated mobile app
  • • No account minimums

Popular choice for long-term investors.

Charles Schwab

  • • Full support for spot crypto ETFs
  • • $0 commissions
  • • Robust IRA/Roth IRA options
  • • Advanced thinkorswim platform available
  • • Extensive educational resources
  • • 24/7 customer support

Great for active traders and retirees.

Vanguard

  • • Supports major Bitcoin & Ethereum ETFs
  • • $0 commissions on most ETFs
  • • Strong focus on low-cost, long-term investing
  • • Excellent retirement account options
  • • Limited altcoin ETF availability (focus on BTC/ETH)

Ideal for buy-and-hold investors.

Robinhood

  • • Supports wide range including XRP & Solana ETFs
  • • $0 commissions
  • • Simple, mobile-first interface
  • • IRA matching program (2026)
  • • Limited research tools

Popular with younger/retail investors.

Interactive Brokers

  • • Advanced trading platform (TWS)
  • • Global market access
  • • Complex interface for beginners

Best for advanced/active traders.

E*TRADE

  • • Comprehensive crypto ETF support
  • • $0 commissions

  • • Good retirement account options
  • • Integration with Morgan Stanley resources

Balanced option for most investors.

Key Considerations When Choosing a Broker

  • • Supported ETFs: Confirm your desired funds (especially XRP/Solana) are available.
  • • Retirement Accounts: Priority if using IRA/Roth for tax advantages.
  • • Fees Beyond Commissions: Inactivity fees, transfer fees, or premium tools.
  • • User Interface: Simple (Robinhood) vs advanced (IBKR).
  • • Customer Support: Important during market volatility.
  • • Security History: All listed have strong records and SIPC insurance.
