Quick Verdict

  • Best Overall / Most Popular: IBIT – Dominates in AUM, liquidity, and trading volume.
  • Best for Fidelity Customers: FBTC – Seamless integration and strong performance.
  • Lowest Fees: ARKB (0.21%) – Ideal for long-term holders focused on minimizing costs.
  • Avoid Unless Legacy: GBTC – High 1.50% fee drives ongoing outflows to cheaper alternatives.

All funds provide nearly identical exposure to spot Bitcoin price (minus fees). The main differences come down to cost, liquidity, and issuer trust.
