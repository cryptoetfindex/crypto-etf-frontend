Pros

  • • From Hashdex, a respected crypto-focused issuer.
  • • Futures-based structure – no direct holding complexities.
  • • Available for investors restricted from spot products.
  • • Reasonable expense ratio for futures category.

Cons

  • • Futures roll costs cause underperformance vs spot ETFs over time.
  • • Small AUM – lower liquidity and wider spreads possible.
  • • Ongoing outflows as spot products dominate.
  • • Higher expense ratio than leading spot funds.

How to Buy DEFI

Available on major brokers including Fidelity, Vanguard, Charles Schwab, Robinhood, and interactive brokers. Search ticker "DEFI". Legacy futures option – generally outperformed by spot ETFs.
