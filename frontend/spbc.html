Portfolio Structure & Holdings (as of late 2025)

SPBC primarily holds low-cost US equity ETFs/futures for broad market exposure and allocates ~10% to spot Bitcoin ETPs (e.g., affiliated or major spot Bitcoin ETFs).

Typical breakdown: ~90-100% in S&P 500/large-cap equity vehicles + 10% Bitcoin ETPs. Actively rebalanced quarterly (or more frequently if needed to cap at 25%). Exact holdings concentrated in a few large positions.

Recent Performance & Flows

  • • Performance in 2025 reflects blended equity + Bitcoin returns, with moderate outperformance vs pure stocks during crypto rallies.
  • • Recent flows: Mixed, with some short-term outflows amid late-2025 market volatility.
  • • Benefits from Bitcoin's strong periods while anchored to stable US equity base.

Pros

  • • Simple way to add targeted Bitcoin exposure to a core US equity portfolio.
  • • Active management handles rebalancing and Bitcoin allocation caps.
  • • Diversified equity base reduces pure crypto volatility.
  • • Potential for enhanced returns when Bitcoin outperforms stocks.

Cons

  • • Higher expense ratio than plain S&P 500 ETFs.
  • • Still exposed to Bitcoin's high volatility (amplified on the 10% allocation).
  • • Smaller AUM and lower liquidity compared to major funds.
  • • Performance drag if Bitcoin underperforms equities.

How to Buy SPBC

Available on major brokers including Fidelity, Vanguard, Charles Schwab, Robinhood, and Interactive Brokers. Search ticker "SPBC". Ideal for investors wanting a balanced stock portfolio with modest Bitcoin upside.
