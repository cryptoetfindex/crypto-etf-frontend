Tips for Minimizing Fees

  • • Choose mini trusts (BTC Mini, ETH Mini) for lowest fees (0.15%).
  • • Use Bitwise or VanEck for competitive 0.20% spot fees.
  • • Avoid Grayscale trusts (2.50%) unless for specific reasons.
  • • For leveraged/income, expect higher fees – use sparingly.
  • • Consider AUM – larger funds often have better liquidity, indirectly reducing costs.
