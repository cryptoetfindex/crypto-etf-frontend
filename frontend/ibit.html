Pros

  • • Highest AUM and liquidity – tight spreads, high volume.
  • • Trusted issuer: BlackRock, world's largest asset manager.
  • • Competitive 0.25% fee.
  • • Easy access via traditional brokerage accounts.

Cons

  • • Exposed to Bitcoin volatility and recent market corrections.
  • • Occasional outflows during risk-off periods.
  • • Custodian risk (though Coinbase is regulated).
  • • No direct Bitcoin ownership (no withdrawals).

How to Buy IBIT

Available on major brokers like Fidelity, Vanguard, Charles Schwab, Robinhood, and eToro. Search ticker "IBIT". Consider tax implications in taxable vs. retirement accounts.
